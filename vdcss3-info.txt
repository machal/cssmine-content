- přidal jsem (embedding permissions in the font's header), což v CZ textu nebylo
**TTF/OTF** (TrueType/OpenType) – these are two formats which are supported by almost all modern browsers, however MSIE supports them from version 9. Furthemore, you have to set the so called „embeding bits“ (embedding permissions in the font's header) to „installable“. [caniuse.com/ttf] (poked to nechceš, dám pryč)
